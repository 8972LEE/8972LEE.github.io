How to begin

  • 强调某个话题一直是争论的焦点，或者强调某个长期存在的现象
  • 讨论题之所以热门，是因为某种趋势，某种事情的发生
  • 定义某个具体的对象或者人，包括特点，功能等

About introducion

  • Background information
    • controversies, hot topic
    • development(technology), trend
    • definition of something, including features
  • thesis statement
    • Always include a concession
  • roadmap
    • clearly show the content of BP1 and BP2

The whole essay

The overall impact of technological development in business has been positive or negative for individual firms.

Introduction

The primary aim for any business is to maximise profits. To meet this aim the increased use of technology has become vital for many businesses. The progress of technology has made a huge difference in business and has rapidly changed the business environment.

Background information: importance and influence of technology

Although there are potential drawbacks to deploying new technology, technology has resulted in benefits which far outweigh the costs by improving efficiency and making products and services more convenient.

worthy to borrow: combine the roadmap with the thesis statement

Concession

It is claimed that technology’s impact on business is negative. The cost of investing in new technological innovations can be a considerable risk for firms.

claim : doubt. 1st sentence could be a generalization, and the second sentence could explain and develop one of the main points.

The risk is illustrated by Input text 1 which shows that more than half of companies have had failures in technology projects over the last five years.

connecting words

the citation way: is illustrated by (xx,xx)

Additionally, funding is required, not only to deploy the new technology, but also to maintain and upgrade the deployments.Hence, a substantial amount of money could be wasted by companies on technology during its development.

writer’s voice Function: deeper explanation, adding points, conclusion

Technology can also waste time for employees, such as with Spam. Spam costs businesses billions of dollars each year because of time wasted.

idea+support

Another serious effect of technology is on brick and mortar stores that suffer as a result of competitors employing technology. Firms such as Nordstrom’s are suffering poor sales because of other businesses using technology.

idea+support

Rebuttal 1

  • efficiency——reasons—–_(1. speed increase; 2. costs decline)

Despite these disadvantages, the main advantage of technology in business is that it increases production efficiency.

link with previous para, focus: increased production efficiency

This is because fewer workers are needed to perform a task, thereby increasing the speed of the task while also reducing the cost of labour. Examples of this include the ability of FedEx to employ fewer delivery workers by developing an automated parcel tracking technology and American Airlines pioneering the use of computers for booking airline tickets, replacing the work of 12 people. Furthermore, alongside increases in efficiency, American Airlines is making it possible to sell more airline tickets to more customers.

examples of this include : a fantastic way to combine two examples

Alongside + previous ideas: link

Rebuttal 2

  • more convenient———–examples

In addition to efficiency, another beneficial aspect of technology is that it can make products and services more convenient, thus making them more accessible and appealing to customers.

link with previous para

thus making: remind result???

For example, Walmart uses inventory technology to provide lower costs for customers, making Walmart a market leader.

Additionally, Amazon has been able to attract nearly 20% of American retail customers over brick and mortar stores because of their convenience and lower costs.

In addition, new technologies have often created new products and new markets, with FedEx and American Airlines being specific examples. In the case of FedEx, their tracking technology became popular with customers, enabling them to track deliveries online; and with American Airlines, their computer booking system, evolved into the global standard for online reservations.

These cases of technological innovation and the increase in internet access have further enabled companies to sell more products and services to an increased number of customers.

wonderful conclusion

In conclusion, it can be seen that while the use of technology has the potential to bring negatives to individual firms, the advantages of using technology significantly outweigh the drawbacks.

stance

Technology can increase potential profits, by increasing production, lowering costs(efficiency) and improving accessibility(convenience).

Body paragraph

Thus, while there are still risks involved, economic benefits are available to those firms who deploy and use technology wisely(word count:528 words)

Positive

Introduction

This generation of America raised in the era of smart phones and the Internet is known as the iGen. As Input Text 1 states, this generation was born between 1995 and 2012.

Definition of IGen

The impact of smart phones on this generation is controversial. Some writers argue that the effect has been harmful but others suggest that these devices have had mainly positive effects.

Controversy: different opinions

This essay will argue that while smart phones have brought some problems relating to mental health, overall, teenagers in the USA have benefited from them and are safer, more responsible and successful than they were in the past.

thesis statement + roadmap

Concession

It has been argued that smart phones cause unhappiness and mental health problems among American teenagers.

Topic sentence

  • unhappiness
  • mental health problems

Research shows that teenagers who use mobile devices most tend to be unhappier than those who use them less(Input Text 1)

Unhappiness: Supporting idea 1

Input Text 1 describes another study which found that student became unhappier as they spent increasing amounts of time on Facebook, but students who were already unhappy did not increase their time using the social network.

Unhappiness: Supporting idea 2

This indicates that using Facebook was directly causing their unhappiness.

Unhappiness: Writer’s conclusion

This is clearly a harmful phenomenon with dangerous effects, such as the growing teenage suicide rate(Input Text 1 ).

However, there is evidence that apps can prevent, rather than cause, mental health problems. Input Text1 describes two different studies showing that special mental health apps have numerous positive effects for teens.

Aside from mental health issue, IGen teenagers are safe and more responsible than teenagers in previous generations. As Input Text 1 states, because they seem to be less interested in driving, today’s teenagers are less likely to die in car accidents. Moreover, IGen teenagers are less likely to be killed by peer. The teenage homicide rete has decreased every year since 2007, the year of the Iphone’s release (Input Text1). One reason for this could be that teenager now interact more virtually than in person, reducing the likelihood of violent fights. These positive trends have happened at the same time as decreases in drug and alcohol use and rates of communicating and getting information about health and wellbeing using smart phones.

Aside from these improvements, there is evidence that smart phones have enabled teenagers in America to be more focused on their future success. According to Input Text 2, a survey found that Ammerican teenagers considered their future careers when thinking about which university course to study. This survey also found that increasing number of teenagers have theor own online business. This illustrates the way that digital technology has encouraged young people to develop their entrepreneurial skills. Successful online business like Flog, Sneakerdon and kidzcationz have been created by teenagers(Input Text 2). It is arguable that without smart phone technology. this would not have been possible.

In conclusion, smart phones have had numerous influences on teenagers’ lives. The American teenagers who have grown up with smart phones are safe, responsible and focused on the future. While valid concerns about mental health have been raised, there is evidence that smart phones can help overcome these problems. In short, despite some problems, the overall impact of smart phones on America teenagers has been positive.
