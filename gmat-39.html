Quantum Theory

Quantum theory, although of tremendous scientific value, has nevertheless prompted debate among physicists. The debate arose because quantum theory addresses the peculiar properties of minute objects such as photons and electrons.

量子理论存在争论，原因：研究的是极微小物体如光子和电子的罕见性质

While one type of experiment shows that these objects behave like particles, with well-defined trajectories through space,

第一种论断： 这些物体行为像粒子，具有明确的空间轨迹

another demonstrates that, on the contrary, they behave waves, their peaks and troughs producing characteristic “interference“ effects.

第二种论断：表现出波动、波峰和波谷，产生典型的“干涉”效应。

However, scientists have failed to devise an experiment to demonstrate both behaviors simultaneously.

然而科学家无法同时用实验证明

In the 1920s, two alternate interpretations of quantum theory attempted to resolve this apparently contradictory wave-particle duality.

两种不同解释 WPD

Physicist Niels Bohr argued that wave-particle properties are not contradictory, but complementary. Contrary to our intuition that an object continues to exist in some determined form even though we cannot perceive it, he concluded that the physical of a quantum object is actually undetermined before the object is observed via experiment.

B：不矛盾，反而互补

Conclusion：在通过实验观察到这个物体之前，量子物体的物理性质实际上是不确定的。

Physicist Werner Heisenberg’s “uncertainty principle,” by contrast, postulated that we cannot precisely determine two complementary properties, such as position and momentum, of a quantum object simultaneously: if we measure an object’s position with absolute certainty, then there is an infinite uncertainty in its momentum, and vice versa. He concluded that although we are limited in our ability to measure objects at the atomic and subatomic levels, their position and momentum are nonetheless defined all along.

W：不能精准测量两种互补特性

  • D The tendency of quantum objects to exhibit well-defined trajectories only at either extremely high or extremely low speeds

  • E The behavior of quantum objects as either particles or waves depending on the type of experiment that is performed

Analyzaiton

  • 先抛出争议和问题（Quantum theory）
  • 分别提出两种不同的解释方法
  • 结构简单，领域术语较难
  • 选项与原文相似度极高，认真观察即可
  • 注意明显信号词， However，conclude，by contrast
