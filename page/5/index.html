Customer loyalty——Concession + Rebuttal

Posted on 2019-04-19 | In gmat | | Visitors:
Words count in article: | Reading time ≈

Customer loyalty programs are attempts to bond customers to a company and its products and services by offering incentives-such as airline frequent flyer programs or special credit cards with valuable benefits-to loyal customers.

现实运用： 顾客忠诚项目

In support of loyalty programs, companies often invoke the “80/20” principle,which states that about 80 percent of revenue typically comes from only about 20 percent of customers.

似乎是被二八理论支持的，这种出现在开篇又很often（大众）的观点，一般都是作者要打击的对象

However, this profitable 20 percent are not necessarily loyal buyers, especially in the sense of exclusive loyalty.

However+especially 开始反驳 Rebuttal 你说是不是出题点！！！！

Studies have demonstrated that only about 10 percent of buyers for many types of frequently purchased consumer goods are 100 percent loyal to a particular brand over a one-year period. Moreover, 100-percent-loyal buyers tend to be light buyers of the product or service.

反驳主调已出，此小段当然是supporting ideas 啦

“Divided loyalty” better describes actual consumer behavior, since customers typically vary the brands they buy. The reasons for this behavior are fairly straightforward: people buy different brands for different occasions or for variety, or a brand may be the only one in stock or may offer better value because of a special deal.

ACTUAL 真实情况是·····+原因（概括性）

Most buyers who change brands are not lost forever; usually, they are heavy consumers who simply prefer to buy a number of brands. Such multi-brand loyalty means that one company’s most profitable customers will probably be its competitors’ most profitable customers as well.

more specific ，deeper explanation，

Still, advocates of loyalty programs contend that such programs are beneficial because the costs of serving highly loyal customers are lower, and because such loyal customers are less price sensitive than other customers.

咋又提已被反驳的观点，可能又是一个concession（大概作者觉得老观点被批判的还不够惨）

It is true that when there are start-up costs, such as credit checks, involved in serving a new customer, the costs exceed those of serving a repeat customer. However, it is not at all clear why the costs of serving a highly loyal customer should in principle be different from those of serving any other type of repeat customer.

It is true that “非常明显的让步句”，永远是为了更好地证明HOWEVER之后的真正BOSS

The key variables driving cost are size and type of order, special versus standard order, and so on, not high-loyalty versus divided-loyalty customers. As for price sensitivity, highly loyal customers may in fact come to expect a price discount as a reward for their loyalty.

解释support啦，真正决定消费的不是忠诚度，回扣首段，经理人想方设法加大忠诚顾客的消费

随着驾驶员需要操作越来越自动化的机器，无论是飞机，还是汽车、火车和轮船的制造商都必须处理同一个难题

随着驾驶员需要操作越来越自动化的机器，无论是飞机，还是汽车、火车和轮船的制造商都面临相同的窘境——如何保障安全？由此引出的一系列问题是本文的focus所在： 传感器向控制系统提供了错误数据的后果？从自动驾驶切换到手动操作的耗时是否致命？警觉下降？，被动疲劳？层出不穷的自动系统，又该信任谁？

SO BACK CONTROL OR NOT？

ONE WAY to tell who made the aircraft you are boarding is to steal a glimpse of the cockpit. A traditional control yoke in front of the pilots suggests a Boeing; a joystick beside each seat, an Airbus. Pilots argue about which system is better; neither is considered safer than the other. Each exemplifies a different approach to a problem that manufacturers of not just aircraft but also cars, trains and ships must grapple with as long as human operators handle increasingly automated machines.

要想知道自己乘坐的飞机是哪家公司出品，一个办法是偷瞄一眼驾驶舱。操纵杆按传统方式放在驾驶员座位前方的是波音，放在驾驶员侧方的是空客。至于哪个系统更好，驾驶员们看法不一，他们也不认为哪个就更安全些。随着驾驶员需要操作越来越自动化的机器，无论是飞机，还是汽车、火车和轮船的制造商都必须处理同一个难题。波音和空客展现了不同的解决方案。

The challenge of what engineers call the “human-machine interface” has tragically gained attention after the crash of an Ethiopian Airlines Boeing 737 MAX 8 on March 10th. Eyewitnesses reported that shortly after departing Addis Ababa, the aircraft climbed and dived repeatedly. Similarities were drawn with a fatal crash in Indonesia in October last year. That time, the pilots of a Lion Air MAX 8 struggled, also soon after take-off, with an automated safety system that erroneously tried to prevent the aircraft from stalling by lowering its nose.

三月 10 日埃塞俄比亚航空公司的一架波音 737 MAX 8 坠毁后，工程师们所说的“人机界面”难题以悲剧性的方式受到了关注。目击者报告称，飞机离开亚的斯亚贝巴后不久反复爬升又俯冲，与印尼去年 10 月发生的致命事故相似。当时，也是在起飞后不久，狮航一架波音 737 MAX 8 客机的驾驶员无法控制自动安全系统，该系统错误地试图压低机头来防止飞机失速。

Although authorities around the world have grounded the model, Boeing insists that it is airworthy. The company is updating the MAX’s automated flight-control software to make it easier for pilots to assume manual control. Boeing and Airbus both pack their planes with computers that do most of the flying. Each, though, espouses a different philosophy on how a pilot reacts to them, says Mudassir Lone of Cranfield University in Britain. Boeings are designed to make the pilot feel like the aviator in charge. Although the control yoke looks and feels like something from the analogue era, the way it behaves—including shaking when approaching a stall—is created digitally by a computer. Airbus’s joystick is seldom used besides take-off and landing. A sound alerts the pilot to trouble; in an Airbus, he is more supervisor than airman.

虽然各国政府已禁飞 MAX 8 客机，但波音公司仍坚称该型号客机是适航的。波音正在升级 MAX 的自动飞行控制软件，方便驾驶员手动接管飞行。波音和空客的飞机都安装了自动驾驶系统，控制着大部分的飞行操作。但英国克兰菲尔德大学（Cranfield University）的穆达希尔·隆内（Mudassir Lone）认为，两者在处理驾驶员如何对自动驾驶系统做出反应上有着不同的理念。波音的设计令驾驶者感觉自己是操控飞机的飞行员。虽然操纵杆的外观和感觉都像是模拟信号时代的产物，但其动作方式（包括在接近失速时的抖动） 是由计算机以数字方式生成的。而在空客的飞机上，除了在起飞和降落时，驾驶员很少需要用到操纵杆。出现问题时，系统会发出警报声通知驾驶员。在空客飞机上，驾驶员更像指挥官，而不是飞行员。

The big worry is what happens if a sensor feeds the flight-control system the wrong data. This might have happened in the Lion Air crash, according to a preliminary report. Something similar downed an Air France Airbus A330 over the Atlantic in 2009: an airspeed sensor iced over and the ensuing loss of data caused the autopilot to disengage. Unable to work out what was happening, the pilots lost control.

人们最担心的是，万一传感器向飞控系统提供了错误数据会有什么后果。一份初步调查报告显示，狮航坠机事件中可能就出现了这种情况。2009 年法航的一架空客 A330 客机也因类似问题坠落大西洋：空速传感器结冰，系统无法读取数据，导致自动驾驶仪解除控制。驾驶员搞不清楚发生了什么情况，没能控制住飞机。

Switching from automatic to manual is not straightforward. Flight-control systems may not disengage entirely. Instead, they might continue to assist the pilot in an attempt to prevent a dangerous manoeuvre. When things do go wrong, it is critical that pilots follow the correct procedures, which are different for each model of aircraft. Pilots learn these and carry checklists spelling them out. Proliferation of systems necessitates frequent retraining. To make life easier for pilots, the MAX 8 employs a system that makes it feel to them like older, more familiar versions of the 737. But this adds another layer of complexity.

从自动驾驶切换到手动操作不是一件简单的事情。飞控系统可能没有完全解除控制。相反，它可能会继续协助驾驶员以防出现危险操作。一旦真出现了问题，驾驶员按正确步骤处理是至关重要的。但每种机型的应对步骤各不相同，驾驶员必须一一学习并带上列明步骤的操作清单。新系统层出不穷，使得驾驶员需要频繁再培训。为方便驾驶员操作，MAX 8 采用的系统在操作感受上沿袭了驾驶员们更熟悉的波音 737 旧机型。但这又增添了一层复杂性。

Incidents are not confined to aviation. In Washington, DC, automated trains have largely been out of service since 2009, when a faulty circuit made a stationary train invisible to the safety system on the one behind it. The driver was unable to brake in time; the resulting crash killed nine people. Ships may soon face similar problems. Some ferries and offshore support vessels have already replaced ship’s wheels with computer-assisted joysticks. A series of accidents involving self-driving cars may have been caused by sensors’ failure to recongnise objects in the road, and drivers failing to respond fast enough.

事故不仅限于航空领域。2009 年，美国华盛顿特区的一辆地铁列车因电路故障造成安全系统失灵，撞上了前方停着的列车。自那以后，该地区的自动驾驶列车已基本停运。当时列车司机无法及时刹车，事故造成九人死亡。船舶也许很快会遇到类似问题。一些渡轮和近海辅助船已经用计算机辅助操纵杆取代舵轮。一系列涉及自动驾驶汽车的事故可能是由于传感器未能识别道路中的物体造成的，而驾驶员来不及反应。

Studies have shown that when people have to wrest control from an automated system, it can take them around five seconds to grasp what is happening. The monotony of monitoring a semi-automated vehicle may reduce vigilance by provoking what psychologists refer to as “passive” fatigue. Such concerns have led some carmakers, Ford among them, to consider skipping semi-automation and go straight to something closer to full autonomy, cutting people out of the loop. That would remove the human-machine interface—but not humans’ machine-induced fears.

研究表明，当人们必须要从自动化系统手上夺回控制权时，可能需要约五秒的反应时间来弄清楚状况。监控半自动驾驶交通工具的那种单调乏味可能会造成心理学家所说的“被动”疲劳，导致驾驶者的警觉性下降。出于这方面的担忧，福特等部分汽车制造商考虑跳过半自动化，直接采用更接近全自动的方式，将人类完全排除在外。人机界面的问题可能会就此消除，但人们因机器引发的恐惧依然存在。

Dim Galaxies

In addition to conventional galaxies, the universe contains very dim galaxies that until recently went unnoticed by astronomers. Possibly as numerous as conventional galaxies, these galaxies have the same general shape and even the same approximate number of stars as a common type of conventional galaxy, the spiral, but tend to be much larger. Because these galaxies’ mass is spread out over larger areas, they have far fewer stars per unit volume than do conventional galaxies. Apparently these low-surface-brightness galaxies, as they are called, take much longer than conventional galaxies to condense their primordial gas and convert it to stars—that is, they evolve much more slowly.

These galaxies may constitute an answer to the long-standing puzzle of the missing baryonic mass in the universe. Baryons—subatomic particles that are generally protons or neutrons—are the source of stellar, and therefore galactic, luminosity, and so their numbers can be estimated based on how luminous galaxies are. However, the amount of helium in the universe, as measured by spectroscopy, suggests that there are far more baryons in the universe than estimates based on galactic luminosity indicate. Astronomers have long speculated that the missing baryonic mass might eventually be discovered in intergalactic space or as some large population of galaxies that are difficult to detect.

The cause of the wreck of the ship Edmund Fitzgerald in a severe storm on Lake Superior is still unknown. When the sunken wreckage of the vessel was found, searchers discovered the hull in two pieces lying close together. The storm‘s violent waves would have caused separate pieces floating even briefly on the surface to drift apart. Therefore, the breakup of the hull can be ruled out as the cause of the sinking.
Which of the following is an assumption on which the argument depends?

A．Ships as large as the Edmund Fitzgerald rarely sink except in the most violent weather.
B．Underwater currents at the time of the storm did not move the separated pieces of the hull together again.
C．Pieces of the hull would have sunk more quickly than the intact hull would have.
D．The waves of the storm were not violent enough to have caused the ship to break up on the surface.
E．If the ship broke up before sinking, the pieces of the hull would not have remained on the surface for very long.

Central ideas

  • 前提：风暴会把分裂的船体（浮在水面上，哪怕只有一点）吹散
  • 推断：沉没在海底的两片船体挨在一起（已知） —> 两片船体没有被风暴吹散（和前提冲突） —> 船体没有分裂（至少在风暴进行的时候）
  • 结论：船体的分裂不是船下沉的原因

Options

  • B 取非削弱原文，若是洋流能将飘散的残片聚拢，那么船的下沉就是由于分裂导致的，只不过洋流将他们聚拢了而已

Good morning, everyone. My presentation today is focusing on methods for car-sharing start-ups in China to reduce cash flow pressure. I’ v divided today ‘s talk into 3 parts. First, I will demonstrate some background information about challenges faced by those companies. Then I ‘ll move on to explain causes behind these challenges. Finally, 3 key solutions and evaluations will be discussed. Now, let’s begin,

Do you notice this number in red? Do you know what it represents? Well, it’s the annual growth expected for the Chinese car-sharing fleet until 2025, according to (Roland berger, 2017). Although It seems like this emerging service is booming, the big danger is below the surface. Recently, many car-sharing companies went bankrupt because of cashflow pressure, such as Togo, Ezzy, ponycar and so on. There is not doubt that the car-sharing as an environmentally friendly way of traveling contributes to the future sustainable development since it greatly improves efficiency and utilization of car using like this picture demonstrates, from 7% to 40% (Accenture,2016). Not only the Chinese government, but also consumers are looking forward to its wide application. So, it is definitely significant for us to consider how to reduce the cash flow pressure faced by car-sharing start-ups. That’s what my presentation will discuss today.

Before considering reasonable solutions, it is better to know what cause cash flow pressure? Well, there are two major categories of costs leading to this pressure. First of all, expensive prices of constituting infrastructure network. As you can see that car-sharing service is more common in developed areas such as Shanghai, Guangzhou, Beijing. However, except great market demand in those areas, expense of providing sufficient charging and parking places is also high, leading to excessive cash flow pressure on car-sharing start-ups.

Aside from infrastructure constitution expense, various after-sale costs are also worried by those companies. For example, material costs, including Loss of keys, fuel cards, vehicle licenses, Also, insurance is a big cost. Moreover, offline operational costs also matter, involving extra cleaning, moving incorrectly parked cars, stolen car tracking and so on. In conclusion, fixed expense including infrastructure constitution and after-sale costs are the main source of cash flow pressure.

Now let’s move on to the third section of my talk – solutions and evaluations. I will focus on three solutions.

The first solution I’m going to talk about is relying on the support policy of government. According to World bank(2011), the government offered financial incentives for the purchase of a NEW Energy Vehicle of up to120,000 RMB. In addition to monetary policies, easier accesses to the necessary operating certificates and preferred parking places are also provided by the government. ( World Bank, 2011). This is likely to be effective solution because adequate money and policy support definitely can decline related costs without spending much. However, one problem is that, is this support really sufficient for this huge industry? The answer is not , In Tier 1 cities, there is still a severe shortage of parking spots for those companies because of the gap between limited government resources and companies’ huge demand . Aside from that, Government subsidy has been continuously declining since 2017. overall, this solution is not adequate to relive excessive cash flow pressure faced by car-sharing start-ups.

The second solution I’m going to discuss is building proper partnerships. Let me show you what it looks like. In terms of car-sharing industry, there is no primary difference in core technology. What matters is users’ experience. If consumers have satisfactory experience, they will continue to pay for this service, thus, better user experience, greater profits, less cash flow pressure. So, who could be partners to offer better user experience? In fact, there are many options. Such as original equipment manufacturers(OEM), which could offer relatively cheap supply of cars. Moreover, they can also take advantage of internet companies’ strong database to improve their services. Besides, local partners and hot spots could also provide sufficient offline support. For example, an integrated car-sharing network covering the main mobility sce­narios, connecting hotels, shopping centers and tour­ist attractions.(Roland Berger, 2017). This solution is highly effective since Such partnerships not only im­prove the user experience,,leading to fewer cash flow pressure but also bring added value—- more choices and special prices to all stakeholders with additional customers for shop­ping centers, tourist attractions. One problem is that it seems difficult for those start-ups to make rational use and balance of these various resources.

The third and final solution is precisely targeting consumers. As the saying goes, you don’t try to eat too much at once. This is the same for car-sharing start-ups. It could be better for them to precisely target on one particular group initially. For example, EZZY provided premium car-sharing services with higher membership fee to young female white collar workers since they are not overly price sensitive (Roland Berger, 2017). Moreover, (Accenture, 2016) suggest that those start-ups could begin with station-based
services for large enterprises only since business users remain the top car-sharing consumers by population and payment. But, could they get accurate user data to precisely target consumers?. It’s true that this service is attractive to particular consumers. But it’s also a long-time taking and big-spending process for those start-ups because of lacking of sufficient resources and capital to obtain such complex and accurate user information. It would be wise for them to collaborate with Internet companies just as the second solution suggests.

In conclusion, I have outlined the problem and suggested three very different solutions. The least effective solution is just relying on the government policy, as it is not sufficient to relieve the huge cash-flow pressure. Precisely targeting consumers could be effective in the long term, but it will definitely aggravate current cash-flow pressure. Thus, for me, the most effective solution to reduce cash-flow pressure is to build proper partnerships—building a whole car-sharing system. Thank

数量增减对比型

Posted on 2019-04-16 | In gmat | | Visitors:
Words count in article: | Reading time ≈

illegal harvest

In 1992 outlaw fishing boats began illegally harvesting lobsters from the territorial waters of the country of Belukia. Soon after, the annual tonnage of lobster legally harvested in Belukian waters began declining; in 1996, despite there being no reduction in the level of legal lobster fishing activity, the local catch was 9,000 tons below pre-1992 levels. It is therefore highly likely that the outlaw fishing boats harvested about 9,000 tons of lobster illegally that year.

Which of the following is an assumption on which the argument depends?

A. The illegal lobster harvesting was not so extensive that the population of catchable lobsters in Belukia’s territorial waters had sharply declined by 1996.

B. The average annual lobster catch, in tons, of an outlaw fishing boat has increased since 1992.

C. Outlaw fishing boats do not, as a group, harvest more lobsters than do licensed lobster-fishing boats.

D. The annual legal lobster harvest in Belukia in 1996 was not significantly less than 9,000 tons.

E. A significant proportion of Belukia‘s operators of licensed lobster-fishing boats went out of business between 1992 and 1996.

Central ideas

  • 92年出现非法捕虾船—-＞合法捕到的龙虾数量减少

  • 96年合法少捕到9000吨龙虾—-＞这9000吨都是96那年非法捕获的

  • 数量增减对比型

  • 结论： 当年出现的非法捕获是导致96年合法捕虾数量减少的唯一原因

Options

  • 92年和96年对比的前提必须是：能够捕到的虾的数量是一样的。基数相同才能进行比较。
  • A：非法捕虾并没有严重到让1996年B水域地虾的数量大幅减少~虾的总量基本保持不变
  • 取非 A： 若92年这边的虾都被捕完了。96年不管有没有非法捕虾，96年虾数量减少，就不是因为那年非法捕捞了。
  • b 1992年不合法的捕获量开始增加。（无关，题目问的是1996年的事情）
  • c 非法的捕获量并不比合法的多（无关的比较。谁多谁少无所谓）
  • d 1996年合法的的捕获量不少于9000吨（无关，合法的捕获了多少与结论无关）
  • e 大部分合法的船在1992年到1996年都不干活（无关，他们不干活不能说明龙虾都被非法的抓走了，并且他们在1992 年到1996年本来就不干活，对合法的和非法的无影响）

Reflection

  • 注意分号，将文章切割为两个大块
  • 由过去推现在，（既然那年发生A，是由B导致的，那么现在发生A也是由B导致的），需保持过去与现在的基本条件一致，才能进行同等推理
