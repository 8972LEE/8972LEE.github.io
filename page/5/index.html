From OG 2019 CR parts

leadership theories

Many leadership theories have provided evidence that leaders affect group success rather than the success of particular individuals. So it is irrelevant to analyze the effects of supervisor traits on the
attitudes of individuals whom they supervise. Instead, assessment of leadership effectiveness should occur only at the group level. Which of the following would it be most useful to establish in order to evaluate the argument?

A. Whether supervisors’ documentation of individual supervisees’ attitudes toward them is usually accurate
B. Whether it is possible to assess individual supervisees’ attitudes toward their supervisors without thereby changing those attitudes
C. Whether any of the leadership theories in question hold that leaders should assess other leaders’ attitudes
D. Whether some types of groups do not need supervision in order to be successful in their endeavors
E. Whether individuals’ attitudes toward supervisors affect group success

此题词汇异常简单，完全能看懂，可是我做错了！！

  • documentation 必备资料，证明文件

原文

  • 领导只影响团队层面的成功，而不影响个人层面的成功，因此不需要研究领导特点对于下属态度的影响

    选项

  • 巧妙转换的E选项: 领导不影响个人层面的成功，并不代表完全不影响个人态度。个人态度受领导影响，且个人态度会影响团队成功，那么就必须研究领导特点对下属态度的影响了。

  • 此种题属于评价题，一般可将疑问语气的选项变为肯定成熟句，看其是否能加强或是反驳原文观点。

health insurance

The rule is intended to save the company the money it might otherwise spend on medically unnecessary procedures. The company has recently announced that in order to reduce its costs, it will abandon this rule.

  • justification
    • 正当理由 a good reason why sth. exists or is done
    • 解释，辩护
  • ailment
    • an illness that is not very serious
    • 程度轻于disease
  • prescribe
    • 给 …开药，开处方
    • 规定，命令，指示
  • review panel
    • panel 专家咨询组
      • an advisorypanel
      • a panel of experts
    • review 表评审（以进行必要的修改）
    • peer-reviewed 同业互检 经过了peer-reviewed 的论文，可信度会高一些
  • physician 内科医生
    • physicist 物理学家
    • surgeon 外科医生
  • only if 只有（相当正式）记得倒装
    • e.g. Only if a teacher has given permission is a student allowed to leave the room.
    • e.g. Only if the red light comes on is there any danger to employees.
    • 区别于 if only（虚拟语气） If only I were rich/If only I knew her name

Which of the following, if true, most helps to explain why deaths and injuries resulting from automobile accidents have not increased significantly?

Which of the following, if true, most logically completes the argument below?

Manufacturers are now required to make all cigarette lighters child-resistant by equipping them with safety levers. But this change is unlikely to result in a significant reduction in the number of fires caused by children playing with lighters, because children given the opportunity can figure out how to work the safety levers and ___.

(A) the addition of the safety levers has made lighters more expensive than they were before the requirement was instituted
(B) adults are more likely to leave child-resistant lighters than non-child-resistant lighters in places that are accessible to children
(C) many of the fires started by young children are quickly detected and extinguished by their parents
(D) unlike child-resistant lighters, lighters that are not child-resistant can be operated by children as young as two years old
(E) approximately 5,000 fires per year have been attributed to children playing with lighters before the safety levers were required

题型 ：观点补充型

  • 题干重点 : 揭示题型
  • 原文重点：
    • but之前是背景介绍（略读），之后是核心内容
    • 防儿童打火机发行后，儿童引发的火灾未– because 他们会卸安全杆 AND？？

选项分析

  • A expensive， 儿童更难买到打火机了

  • B CORRECT 儿童引发火灾 = 他们会用打火机AND拥有打火机

  • C 被家长没收了打火机，怎么放火？

  • D 十分无关

  • E 之前普通打火机造成的火灾与现在防儿童打火机发行后造成的火灾有什么关系

PhishCo runs a number of farms in the arid province of Nufa, depending largely on irrigation. Now, as part of a plan to efficiently increase the farms’ total production, it plans to drill down to an aquifer containing warm, slightly salty water that will be used to raise fish in ponds. The water from the ponds will later be used to supplement piped-in irrigation water for PhishCo’s vegetable fields, and the ponds and accompanying vegetation should help reduce the heat in the area of the farms.

Which of the following would, if true, most strongly suggest that the plan, if implemented, would increase the overall efficiency of PhishCo’s farms?

  • A Most of the vegetation to be placed around the ponds is native to Nufa.
  • B Fish raised on PhishCo’s farms are likely to be saleable in the nearest urban areas.
  • C Organic waste from fish in the pond water will help to fertilize fields where it is used for irrigation.
  • D The government of Nufa will help to arrange loan financing to partially cover the costs of drilling.
  • E Ponds will be located on low-lying land now partially occupied by grain crops.

题型 ：方法——目的类

  • 题干重点：increase the overall efficiency

    • 效率高=少花费+高产出

原文重点: a plan to efficiently increase farm’s productivity

PLAN ：

  • 挖井来养鱼并灌溉农场
  • 植物和池塘降农场温度

选项分析

A. 种植当地作物比非当地作物能提高效率吗？

当地典型植物应该耐旱，而本文的蔬菜在池塘边

B. saleable 意味着更多利润而非更高效率

C. CORRECT 鱼塘肥既减少花费又提高产量；效率++

D.政府财政补贴与效率相干吗？

E.与A类似，种谷物就比蔬菜产量效率高吗？
