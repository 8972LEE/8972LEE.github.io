Learn Vocabulary from CR 5

Posted on 2019-04-08 | In gmat | | Visitors: |
Words count in article: | Reading time ≈

词汇这种东西，一定要日更，大家监督我呀。

其实UP一直被单词问题困扰了许久，曾经也按照杨鹏老师的方法《17天记忆GRE单词》进行过一个半月，背完了六级和雅思，虽然单词掌握数量确实增加了不少，可是灵活运用却是一大BUG。之后又因偶然机会听到了曲根老师讲授的词根词缀记忆法，真真是人类的本质是重复，通过一个母词衍生出的各种相关词汇，反复记忆前缀词源，并时不时地趣味联想，记忆会变得异常牢固，遂萌发将词根词缀法与词汇记忆更深度的结合——在语境里背单词，90%的题做错还真不是因为逻辑，而是因为词汇理解能力。

From OG 2019 CR parts

drug-taking

Any honest proponent of legalization would be wise to assume that drug-taking as a whole would rise

  • proponent 支持者{pro-表推进的前缀e.g. promote，profound，process }
    • 形近意近： prophet （预言者，倡导者，拥护者）
    • SYN： supporter， advocate（作名词时直接表示倡导者，无须加r），
    • OPP： opponent，objector（op- ob表反向）
    • OPP： discommender， dissenter（dis- 表否定，联assent）

Food scarcity

Which of the following most logically completes the reasoning ?

Either food scarcity or excessive hunting can threaten a population of animals（总起）. If the group faces food scarcity, individuals in the group will reach reproductive maturity later than otherwise. If the group faces excessive hunting, individuals that reach reproductive maturity earlier will come to predominate（两句皆support首句）. Therefore, it should be possible to determine whether prehistoric mastodons became extinct because of food scarcity or human hunting, since there are fossilized mastodon remains from both before and after mastodon population declined（有化石供勘探）, and —

原文大意

  • therefore 之前是有关食物供给量影响种群数目的理论介绍，为后文提供理论基础
  • therefore 之后是 为测定M灭亡提供证据
    • 一. therefore 之前的理论支撑，证明灭绝同食物供给量有关
    • 二 证明理论可行性 1. 有化石 2. 化石提供了所需证据：？？？
  • 因为食物供给量会影响一个种族的生存，所以可以根据？？？和现存的化石研究来研究M的灭绝与否
  • 答案必定与食物供给此概念有关

始纠结于 B C 选项

B the average age at which mastodons from a given period reached reproductive maturity can be established from their fossilized remains

达到生殖成熟期的时间不就是受食物供给的影响吗？ Bingo！

C it can be accurately estimated from fossilized remains when mastodons became extinct

知道何时灭绝，并不能证明 ”根据食物供给多少来判定灭绝与否 “ 此方法的可行性

  • scarcity 短缺

    • water scarcity 水资源短缺； a time of scarcity 物资短缺时间
    • SYN：short of，lack of
    • deficiency，insufficient，shortage

  • reproductive maturity 生育成熟

    • reproductive：生殖的

      ​ re+ productive（高成效的）（fruitful（果实累累的）， prolific 多产的 ）

  • predominate 占优势，占主导地位（adj & n）

    • pre（在前） + dominate（主导）
    • pre（早于-前缀）来源于 previous

  • prehistoric mastodons

    • pre（在前）+historic 史前时期

    • mastodon 乳齿象 简化为M即可

  • extinct 绝迹的，绝种的 联 extinguish【V】

Toxic substances

Many office buildings designed to prevent outside air from entering have been shown to have elevated levels of various toxic substances circulating through the air inside, a phenomenon known as sick syndrome . Yet the air in other office building does not have elevated levels of these substances, even though those buildings are the same age as the “sick buildings and have similar designs and ventilation systems”. This may because that certain adhesives and drying agents used in particular types of furniture, carpets, and paint contribute the bulk of the toxic substances that circulate in the air of office buildings.

  • elevated 程度高的 SYN： raised， enhanced， heightened

    • Other meanings ： 高尚的 SYN： noble

    • SYN： magnificent（magnificent obsession 情定三生）

  • toxic substances 有毒物质

    • toxic SYN： poisonous

    • substances 物质 ，实质 in substance == in essence

  • syndrome 综合症状

    • syn（同，一起）+drome（跑）——一起发作—–综合症状
    • 程度：aliment（小病）＜disease ＜syndrome

  • ventilation 通风设备

    • ventilator 换气扇

  • adhesives 粘合剂

    • SYN： bindings

  • agent 除了表代理还可以表药剂

    • drying agent； wetting agent

  • bulk 表体积，容量

    • the bulk of 表大多数
